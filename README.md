# CareerCopilotAI

> AI-powered Career Operating System for Job Seekers and Recruiters

## Overview

CareerCopilotAI is an AI-powered platform that helps professionals throughout their career journey.

Unlike traditional resume builders or ATS checkers, CareerCopilotAI combines resume intelligence, ATS optimization, job matching, interview preparation, cover letter generation, and career coaching into a single platform.

---

## Vision

To become the most intelligent AI career assistant that helps millions of professionals build successful careers.

---

## Core Features

### Resume Intelligence
- Upload PDF/DOCX resumes
- Extract structured information
- AI-generated summaries

### ATS Analyzer
- Resume vs Job Description comparison
- Keyword analysis
- ATS score
- Improvement recommendations

### Resume Optimizer
- AI resume rewriting
- Better bullet points
- Stronger professional summaries

### Cover Letter Generator
- Personalized cover letters
- Company-specific customization

### Interview Coach
- HR interview questions
- Technical interview questions
- Behavioral interview preparation

### Career Coach
- Career guidance
- Skill gap analysis
- Learning recommendations
